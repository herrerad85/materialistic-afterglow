Afterglow is a modern fork of the Materialistic Hacker News client for Android (https://github.com/hidroh/materialistic).

Features:
- Browse the top, new, best, ask, show and jobs stories on Hacker News
- Read and post comments
- Search stories using Algolia Hacker News Search
- Save stories and comments for offline reading
- Multiple themes including light, dark, and AMOLED dark
- Read article content via the built-in web view
- Share stories and comments
- Supports Android custom tabs for opening links

Uses the official Hacker News API. User actions such as voting, commenting, and submitting stories require a Hacker News account.
